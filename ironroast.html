Iron Roast

Made for Metalheads

Unleash your inner metalhead

Iron Roast is a small coffee company located in central Florida. It pitches itself by delivering unique coffee flavors not commonly seen. However, their original packaging was non-distinct and poorly communicated who their intended audience was. Therefore, a complete overhaul of brand packaging and voice was essential. Moreover, to boost online presence, webpages were revamped and rewritten entirely with a focus on SEO optimization.

Work Summary

Ultimately, we settled on playing into the metal and punk themes by creating labels and designs with an edge to them. Iron Roast has always wanted to set itself up as a brand that fights the system, demonstrated by their pledge to donate 10% of all profits to the Innocence Project.

We also decided to create a motif and eye-catching theme that would make their packages instantly stand-out so you would say "Ah, that's Iron Roast!" The result was the repeating 'Fe' symbol.

Rebranding Summary:

Goal: Enhance brand recognition among coffee metal & punk aficionados who prioritize premium quality in their coffee experience. 

Target: Attract a new audience by redefining Iron Roast as a distinctive brand that is making an impact.

Define: Position Iron Roast as a brand that stands out with its unique, character-rich flavor profiles, distinguishing itself in the coffee industry.

Methodology: Enhance the brand's identity through striking design, inventive naming, and a comprehensive rebranding initiative to infuse Iron Roast with a memorable personality. Establish a clear goal and way of drawing attention and doing good.
